How to prevent virus

Wash your place

Keep your workspace neat and clean so that no virus can enter there.

Maintain Distance

The main spread of virus is caused by standing near a covid infected person. SO try maintaining distance from even a non infected person

don't touch face

Tocuhing your face can lead to entry of virus inside your body easily. The spread can be from anywhere to try to keep lesss contact with your face.

wash your hand

This is a compulsary thing. The germs sitting on your hands or the virus present is killed when you sanitize of wash your hands so always wash your hands.

use napkin

Always use a napkin to take food or hand over something to any person as the virus can spread from anywhere even your hand touching anything.

wear a mask

Always wear a mask to prevent entry of virus through talking or even in coming in contact with a covid infected person.
